# ComandaJá - Product Requirements Document

## Overview
Premium digital menu and QR ordering platform for snack bars and small restaurants in Brazil.

## Brand Positioning
"Modernize seu lanchonete com um cardápio digital premium e experiência de pedido mobile."

## Design Principles
- Minimalist Premium
- Modern SaaS (Stripe/Notion/Apple inspired)
- Clean & Polished
- Mobile-First Customer Experience
- Elegant Admin Dashboard

## Screens to Generate

### Customer Experience (Mobile Focus)
1. **Public Menu**: Header (logo, banner, status), Category nav, Product cards.
2. **Product Detail Modal/Page**: Image, quantity, observations, add to cart.
3. **Cart & Checkout**: Product list, subtotal, customer info (WhatsApp), order type (table/pickup/delivery).
4. **Order Confirmation**: Summary with "Enviar pedido via WhatsApp" button.

### Restaurant Admin Dashboard (Desktop Focus)
5. **Dashboard Overview**: Metrics, recent orders, QR access, open/closed toggle.
6. **Orders Management**: Order cards/table with statuses (Novo, Preparando, Pronto, Finalizado).
7. **Product Management (CRUD)**: List/Grid of products with availability toggles and category filtering.
8. **QR Code & Links**: Preview and download section for restaurant marketing.
